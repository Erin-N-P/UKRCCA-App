RULES

  • A Ready To Run (RTR) or competition RC Crawler with 2.4ghz transmitter and receiver is required.
  • Vehicle touches (including any repairs or repositions) will be given an automatic 10 points, unless accidental.
  • On Course Repairs must be completed on course, and completed within the course time.
  • Off Course Repairs can be called when deemed acceptable. These should be complete within 30 minutes. If completed on time, a 1 minute penalty is given and the driver returns to the previous gate cleared.
  • If at any point the Judge has reason to believe the vehicle is out of spec, they may call time to stop. If the vehicle is determined to have an unfair advantage, a repair must be done to correct the problem. If this is impossible, the driver is marked with a DNF.
  • 10 points are given when 4 wheels do not travel through the gate marker or any part of the vehicle touches the gate marker.
  • 10 points are given when any part of the vehicle touches a boundary marker, and the vehicle is moved back to the previously cleared gate.
  • The maximum amount of points per course is 100. If surpassed, the driver is given a DNF and the vehicle should be removed from the course.
  • DNFs should be given at any time a driver cannot complete a course. A DNS should be given when the course cannot be started.
  • A driver may call for a time pause in order to reposition themselves safely on the course.
  • Completing gates in the unintended order will result in a 10 point penalty.
